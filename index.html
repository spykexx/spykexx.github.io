what we do

We are a Direct Primary Care (DPC) clinic servicing the Quad Cities area. Our patients pay a flat monthly rate and we do not bill insurance. The care our patients receive is the hightest quality and personalized to their needs (we provide the highest quality primary care available). The monthly fee is more affordable that insurance premiums, deductibles and co-pays. By taking insurance and politics out of healthcare, you and your family will get the care you deserve, when you deserve it.
